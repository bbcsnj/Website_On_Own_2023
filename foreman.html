Biography

George Foreman won the Gold Medal in the Olympics after only a short career as an amateur. His amateur record was 22-4 before he became a professional. Foreman known for his heavy hands, knocked out many opponents on his way up the heavyweight rankings. 68 of his 76 wins were by knockout. He is known for retiring from boxing with an amazing career and a heavyweight championship, only to return at the age of 38. He was at the time 10 years out of the ring. He was able to fight on to win another heavyweight championship at the age of 45. At the age of 48, he finally retired.

George Foreman is known for having not only an amazing career in boxing, but also a lot of success with his life's adventures like his Foreman grill. Foreman has twelve children. His five sons he all named George. While his fight with Muhammad Ali in Africa was legendary he lost, but still continued to make comebacks. This resilience is what Muhammed George Foreman a one of a kind.
