Biography

Livingstone wasn't known for his great record. He was known for his great moment and tremendous heart in the ring. He became the first world champion from Saint Kitts and Nevis. His ascent to his fights with Ray "Boom Boom" Mancini led to his great moment. After this, he would lose his title and become sort of a gate keeper for the lightweight division. This led him to be both a dangerous opponent with every win being earned for his opponents.

Livingstone followed his career by becoming a marathon runner. He signs autographs and tries to live a healthy lifestyle.

Notable opponents:

  • Ray Mancini
  • Roger Mayweather
  • Oba Carr

Famous Quote:

"Mental health has got to be the biggest battle I've ever fought, more so than any opponent."
